Third Section
Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ'S)

What is Roku Express 4K+?

Roku Express 4K+ is a streaming player that lets you enjoy channels like Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, and YouTube, right on your TV and in stunning picture quality. Every Roku player, including Roku Express 4K+, lets you access a massive selection of free, live, and premium TV, so you can watch the entertainment you want without needing to pay for cable. Roku Express 4K+ also features dual-band Wi-Fi® for smooth streaming, even with multiple devices on your network, and a voice remote with TV controls.

What is 4K streaming?

4K describes the picture quality you can get while you’re streaming, as long as you have a 4K compatible TV. With 4x the pixels as standard HD picture quality, 4K is crisp and clear so you’ll experience lifelike detail. Plus with High Dynamic Range (HDR), you’ll experience brighter colors that pop and sharp contrast.

Can you use a 4K streaming device on a non-4K TV?

You can use a 4K streaming device, like Roku Express 4K+, on a non-4K TV as long as it has an HDMI port, but you’ll only be able to stream in HD picture quality. Even if you don’t have a 4K TV, you can still enjoy smooth streaming with Roku Express 4K+ thanks to its dual-band Wi-Fi® connection, plus a voice remote that features TV controls.
